# Sample Contracts

Place sample PDF contracts here for testing.

## Recommended Test Documents

Use publicly available contract templates for testing:

- **NDA Template** - Non-disclosure agreement
- **Service Agreement** - Master service agreement
- **Employment Contract** - Standard employment terms

## Data Sources

Public domain contract templates can be found at:

- [SEC EDGAR](https://www.sec.gov/cgi-bin/browse-edgar?action=getcompany&type=10-K) - Public company filings
- [LawDepot](https://www.lawdepot.com/) - Free contract templates
- [eForms](https://eforms.com/) - Legal document templates
